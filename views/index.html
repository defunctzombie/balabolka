balabolka: (n). person who talks incessantly

Every website should have chat!

Your visitors don't deserve to live in social isolation! You can improve website and user interaction just be having a simple chat box. This allows users to interact with each other and your website in new ways.

Balabolka makes it super easy to add this feature to your website without worry. Just add a these lines to your page and you are set! 

<link rel="stylesheet" href="//{{hostname}}/css/chat.css"/>
<script src="//{{hostname}}/js/chat.js"></script>

How it works

Any users to your site will be presented with a chat dialog like the one seen below. They will be assigned a random name and be able to chat immediately with other users on the site.

The chat messages are all routed through out servers and you don't need to worry about managing the user connections or client side code.

Customize

You can customize the chat window for your site. Just provide your own stylesheet with the following styles after our chat.css:

balabolka-window
the chat window
balabolka-title
chat title
balabolka-messages
messages window
balabolka-message
single chat message
